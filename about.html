Our story

Wallin Labs was started by a software engineer who kept seeing the same problem across different industries: organisations sitting on vast amounts of valuable information, all of it locked inside documents, with no practical way to extract and act on it at scale. Fund managers buried in pitch decks. Journalists with leaked archives no one has time to read. Contractors rewriting the same proposal for the hundredth time.

Off-the-shelf AI tools are built for the average use case. They fit no one's workflow particularly well, and the gaps are always where the value is. Enterprise document platforms exist, but they are slow to deploy, expensive to license, and require months of integration before they are useful. Most teams end up doing the work manually — or not at all.

We built Wallin Labs to close that gap. We design and build custom AI document intelligence tools, scoped to your workflow and integrated with your stack. Our three products — FundMemo, Surfaced, and Ryder — are examples of what that looks like in practice. They started as custom builds for specific problems. The core of the business is doing the same for yours.
