Why we exist

Most AI tools are built for the average use case. They are flexible enough to demo well and rigid enough to disappoint in production. We started Wallin Labs because we kept seeing the same problem: organisations with valuable, complex document workflows and no tool that could handle them properly.

We are software engineers first. We design and build the full pipeline from scratch, around how your team actually works. Ingestion, parsing, analysis, structured output. No resold platforms, no thin wrappers, no compromises.

FundMemo, Surfaced, and Ryder are tools we built to solve real problems for real clients. Proof of what we do, not the limit of it.
